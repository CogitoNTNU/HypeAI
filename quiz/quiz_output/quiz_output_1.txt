1. What event marked the beginning of the French Revolution in 1789?
   - Answer: The convocation of the Estates General.
   
2. Which date is commonly associated with the Storming of the Bastille during the French Revolution?
   - Answer: July 14th.
   
3. Who was the French monarch that was executed during the Revolution in January 1793?
   - Answer: Louis XVI.
   
4. What period of intense political repression in France during the Revolution led to around 16,000 executions?
   - Answer: The Reign of Terror.
   
5. In what year did the French Consulate come to power, marking the end of the French Revolutionary period?
   - Answer: 1799.
